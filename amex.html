How to Use AI for Amex Perk Optimization

Many premium credit card perks go unused each year due to complex tracking and redemption processes. Follow this step-by-step guide to use AI for making sure you maximize all your eligible Amex benefits — saving money and getting full value from your card.

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Download Your Amex Statement
    • Log in to your American Express account on the website or app.
    • Go to your recent statements.
    • Download the statement as a PDF file to your device.
  2. Choose an AI Chatbot with PDF Upload Support
    • Pick an AI assistant (e.g., Perplexity, ChatGPT Plus) that allows PDF uploads.
    • Confirm it can read and analyze documents for perks and credits.
  3. Upload Your Statement
    • Open the chatbot interface and use the “Upload” or “Attach File” button to upload your statement PDF.
  4. Ask the AI to Analyze Your Perks
    • Type a prompt such as:
    • Can you check this Amex statement and see if I’m using all my eligible perks and credits?
    • or
    • Am I maximizing all potential benefits and statement credits based on this statement?
  5. Review the AI’s Response
    • The AI will let you know which perks you’ve used and which you may be missing out on.
    • Look for suggestions for unused credits or underutilized benefits.
  6. Take Action
    • Redeem any unused perks or credits as suggested by the AI.
    • Set reminders for recurring monthly or annual credits.
  7. Optional – Automate or Track Over Time
    • Request the AI to create a monthly checklist or reminder for perk deadlines.
    • Some AI tools can output a spreadsheet for long-term tracking and optimization.
Tip: Choose AI services you trust with sensitive financial documents. Never share statements with unrecognized or untrusted apps.
